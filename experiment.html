Experiment 1

Fixation Accuracy

9 static target dots appear one by one. Look at each dot steadily. WebGazer measures gaze accuracy.

Experiment 2

Reading Task

A paragraph shown word by word. Look at each highlighted word. WebGazer compares gaze accuracy during natural reading.

Experiment 3

Off-Screen Robustness

Look outside the screen in 4 directions for 5 s each. Counts how many gaze estimates land on-screen — fewer is better.

Experiment 4

Cognitive Load

Easy counting vs. serial-7 subtraction. Pupils dilate under mental effort — measures PIR (pupil-to-iris ratio) across task difficulty levels. No gaze calibration needed.

Experiment 5

Luminance Response

Screen cycles grey → white → grey → black → grey. Pupils constrict in bright light and dilate in darkness — validates the pupil size estimator via the pupillary light reflex.
